II. Who We Are

Driven by purpose.

At Sovereign Agricultural Group, our mission is to empower and sustain American family farms and ranches by providing strategic private equity investment while championing sustainable and regenerative practices. In the face of increasing pressure from foreign investors, we are dedicated to preserving the integrity and independence of these vital components of our nation’s agricultural heritage.

Through our investments, we not only safeguard the livelihoods of farming families but also ensure the long-term health and resilience of our food systems. By fostering innovation and implementing environmentally responsible practices, we strive to create a brighter future for agriculture, local communities, and the American farmer.

“Since 2023, the number of American family farms has fallen by 7%. Every year, family farms are lost to foreign investors.”

III. How We Operate

Stewards of the land, empowering American farmers.

Sovereign Agricultural Group recognizes the vital role American farmers play in feeding the nation and preserving our agricultural heritage. However, we also understand the challenges facing these families. Land prices, competition, and economic pressures can threaten the future of independent farms. Our mission goes beyond simply increasing yields. We’re committed to keeping American farms in the hands of American farmers for generations to come.

i.

Empowering Independence

Equipping farmers with the knowledge, resources, and market access needed to remain independent and competitive.

ii.

Succession Planning

Supporting farm families in developing successful succession plans and profitable exit strategies.

iii.

Market Access

Connecting producers with fair-trade markets and distribution channels that capture a larger share of profit.

IV. Core Areas of Investing

Economic viability for long-term success.

01

Agribusiness

Agribusiness undertakes a multifaceted role in the agricultural sector. From cultivating crops to processing and distributing food products, our responsibilities span the entire agricultural value chain, ensuring efficiency and sustainability while navigating market dynamics.

02

Distribution and Logistics

Investments in agricultural technology, precision tools, data analytics, and automation, alongside the transportation infrastructure that streamlines the movement of products from farm to consumer.

03

Farming and Crop Production

Row crops, specialty crops, and organic or sustainable farming practices that align with growing consumer demand for environmentally conscious agriculture.

04

Food Production

Partnering with companies that transform raw materials into consumable food products, including vertical farming and indoor agriculture in controlled environments.

05

Land Acquisitions and Management

Investing in farmland across various states, owning and operating farms, and leasing land to farmers in exchange for a share of the profits.

06

Ranching and Livestock Production

Cattle ranches, dairy farms, and other livestock operations, with an emphasis on responsible animal management and environmental considerations.
