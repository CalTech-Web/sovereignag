IV. How It Works

A simple 4-step process.

Step 1

Initial Contact and Confidentiality Agreement

Overview: Begin by reaching out to Sovereign AG to express your interest in selling your business. This is a crucial step to start the dialogue and protect your sensitive information.

Actions: Contact Sovereign AG via our website or phone to express your interest. Sign a Confidentiality Agreement provided by Sovereign AG, ensuring any proprietary information you share during negotiations remains confidential.

Tips: Prepare basic information about your business to facilitate initial discussions. Ensure to have basic financials and be prepared to answer questions during an initial discovery call.

Step 2

Business Evaluation and Documentation

Overview: Sovereign AG will evaluate your business to understand its value and potential. You will need to provide detailed documentation to support this assessment.

Actions: Submit financial statements, including recent balance sheets, profit and loss statements, and cash flow reports. Prepare operational information, including details about operations, assets, and key contracts. Respond to due diligence requests.

Tips: Ensure all documentation is accurate and up-to-date. Be responsive and cooperative to facilitate a smooth evaluation process.

Step 3

Negotiation and Agreement

Overview: Once the evaluation is complete, you will negotiate the terms of the sale, including the price and any conditions.

Actions: Review the initial offer Sovereign AG presents based on their evaluation. Negotiate the price, payment structure, and any other terms. Finalize the agreement once both parties agree on the terms.

Tips: Consider hiring an attorney or financial advisor to help you understand the terms. Make sure the agreement reflects all aspects of the deal accurately.

Step 4

Closing the Deal

Overview: Finalize the sale and transfer ownership of your business to Sovereign AG. This step includes legal and financial formalities to conclude the transaction.

Actions: Sign the sale agreement to formalize the sale. Transfer ownership by completing the legal paperwork and handing over business assets. Receive payment as per the terms of the agreement.

Tips: Work with your lawyer to handle the legal aspects of the transfer. Make sure all debts and liabilities are addressed before the final transfer.
