HOW IT WORKS!

How to Sell Your Business to Sovereign AG: A Simple 4-Step Process

Overview:
Begin by reaching out to Sovereign AG to express your interest in selling your business. This is a crucial step to start the dialogue and protect your sensitive information.

Actions:

  • Contact Sovereign AG via our website or phone to express your interest.
  • Sign a Confidentiality Agreement provided by Sovereign AG. This ensures that any proprietary information you share during the negotiations remains confidential.

Tips:

  • Prepare basic information about your business to facilitate initial discussions.
  • Ensure to have basic financials and be prepared to answer questions during an initial discovery call.

Overview:
Sovereign AG will evaluate your business to understand its value and potential. You will need to provide detailed documentation to support this assessment.

Actions:

  • Submit Financial Statements: Provide recent balance sheets, profit and loss statements, and cash flow reports.
  • Prepare Operational Information: Share details about your business operations, assets, and key contracts. Identify key personnel and decision makers.
  • Respond to Due Diligence Requests: Sovereign AG may request additional information to perform a thorough assessment.

Tips:

  • Ensure all documentation is accurate and up-to-date.
  • Be responsive and cooperative to facilitate a smooth evaluation process.

 

Overview:
Once the evaluation is complete, you will negotiate the terms of the sale, including the price and any conditions.

Actions:

  • Review the Initial Offer: Sovereign AG will present a preliminary offer based on their evaluation.
  • Negotiate Terms: Discuss and negotiate the price, payment structure, and any other terms with Sovereign AG.
  • Finalize the Agreement: Once both parties agree on the terms, a formal sale agreement is drafted.

Tips:

  • Consider hiring an attorney or financial advisor to help you understand the terms.
  • Make sure the agreement reflects all aspects of the deal accurately.

Overview:
Finalize the sale and transfer ownership of your business to Sovereign AG. This step includes legal and financial formalities to conclude the transaction.

Actions:

  • Sign the Sale Agreement: Formalize the sale by signing the final agreement.
  • Transfer Ownership: Complete the legal paperwork to transfer ownership and hand over business assets.
  • Receive Payment: Ensure you receive the agreed payment as per the terms of the agreement.

Tips:

  • Work with your lawyer to handle the legal aspects of the transfer.
  • Make sure all debts and liabilities are addressed before the final transfer.
